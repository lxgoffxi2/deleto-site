Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Deleto ("the app," "we," "us") handles your information. Deleto is operated by Alexandro Gomez. We built Deleto to be private by design: your photos and videos stay on your device, and we do not collect or sell your personal content.

Photos and Videos

Deleto lets you capture photos and videos that automatically delete on a timer you choose. This media is stored on your own device — saved into a dedicated "DELETO" album or folder in your phone's gallery. We do not upload, transmit, store, or have any access to your photos or videos. They never leave your device through Deleto, and they are permanently deleted from your device when their timer expires or when you delete them manually.

Camera and Microphone

Deleto requests access to your camera (and microphone, for recording video with sound) solely to let you capture media inside the app. This access is used only while you are actively using the camera. We do not record or access your camera or microphone in the background.

Photo Library Access

Deleto requests permission to save to and manage media in your photo library so it can store your captures in the DELETO album and delete them when their timer expires. This permission is used only for the app's core auto-delete function.

Optional Email Backup

Deleto includes an optional feature to email a copy of a photo or video to yourself before it is deleted. If you choose to use this feature, the media is sent using your own email configuration. This is entirely optional and off unless you set it up.

Advertising

Deleto displays ads through Google AdMob to support the free version of the app. Google AdMob may collect certain device and usage information (such as a device advertising identifier) to serve and measure ads. This data is handled by Google in accordance with its own policies. You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. On iOS, Deleto will ask for your permission before any tracking, and you may decline.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or sell your photos or videos.
  • We do not require an account or ask for your name, email, or password.
  • We do not track your location.

Children's Privacy

Deleto is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at deletocam@gmail.com.
